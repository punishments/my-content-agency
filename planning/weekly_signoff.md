## How to Use

1. The Creative Director populates this sheet when all 6 proposals are READY FOR APPROVAL
2. You review each row — open the linked proposal file for full detail when needed
3. Mark your decision for each post: ✅ APPROVED / 🔁 REVISION / ❌ REJECTED
4. Any REVISION or REJECTED decision requires a note — the team needs to know what to change
5. Once all 6 decisions are made, the Copywriter begins on approved posts

**No scripting begins until your decision is logged here.**
