## Section 1 — Idea Summary

**What is this content about?**
A behind-the-scenes look at running a 7-role content agency structure as a single person, using Claude. The hook is the necessity — not the cleverness. Built because there was no team and no budget, not to be impressive. Still imperfect. But it means showing up to a system instead of a blank page.

**Source:** Lived experience / current working practice

### Hooks

**[Framework — Self-Recognition]**
"If you've ever looked at someone's content output and thought 'there's no way they're doing this alone' — I want to show you what it actually looks like, and be honest about where it's still breaking."

**[Adaptive — POV]**
"POV: You're the creative director, the writer, the strategist, the editor, and the person asking yourself if any of this is actually working — all in the same afternoon."

**[Adaptive — Internal Voice]**
"🧠: I can't afford to hire anyone. But I also can't afford to keep doing this without a system."

**Producer notes:**
Internal Voice hook opens the VO — it sets stakes immediately and doesn't require the creator to be on screen. The VO script should feel unpolished — don't over-edit. The hesitation and self-correction mid-script are features, not bugs.
