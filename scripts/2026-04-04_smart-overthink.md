## Caption

That thought has kept more smart people stuck than lack of skill ever did.

It's not weakness. Your brain generates more options, more objections, more scenarios per second than average. That's useful until it isn't.

After a certain point, more thinking isn't analysis. It's delay.

The fix isn't to think less. It's to notice when you've already crossed the line.
