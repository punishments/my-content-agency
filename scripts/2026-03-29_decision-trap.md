## Script

POV: You're 27, you know exactly what you want, and you still can't make a single clean decision.

Here's what's happening.

When you feel behind — like time has already passed and you're running out of it — every decision starts to feel bigger than it is.

Because subconsciously, the choice isn't just the choice anymore. It's the choice that has to make up for the time you think you lost.

So you don't just pick a gym routine. You pick the gym routine that proves you're serious now.

You don't just start a project. You start the project that fixes the last three years.

And when something has to carry that much weight, you can't move it. You just stand there, holding it.

The way out isn't pressure. It's not "just decide."

It's permission to make a small choice that doesn't have to save anything.

One direction. Not the right one. Just one.

Because the people who look like they have it together — they didn't make better decisions than you. They just stopped waiting for the decision to fix the delay.

That's the whole difference.
