# Consistency Isn't the Problem, Trust Is

## Hook (Visual Text)

> 🧠: if this was working, I'd feel it by now.

---

## Caption

That thought ends more streaks than laziness ever did.

Most people don't quit because they're undisciplined. They quit because they don't believe the process is working — even when it is.

The reps are happening. The doubt is just louder.

That's not failure. That's just early.

Build the system. Then trust it long enough to find out.

Follow if you're building something that hasn't shown results yet.
