## Hook (Visual Text)

> POV: You want a different life. You're still waiting for permission to have it.

---

## Caption

The permission isn't coming.

Not because it's being withheld — because no one else can give it. If you don't move until someone says go, failure isn't really your call. That feels safer. It also keeps you stuck indefinitely.

The door only opens from the inside.
