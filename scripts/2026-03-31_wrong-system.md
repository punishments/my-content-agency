**Frame 14**

Different architecture.

## Production Notes

- **Visual anchor:** Phone notes app showing handwritten or typed goal list — this is the hero visual
- **Font:** Clean sans-serif, white on dark background — consistent throughout
- **Music:** Slow ambient build, no beats — music should swell slightly into frames 13–15
- **Duration target:** 22–30 seconds — time text holds to music, not to fixed count
- **Edit note:** Pause on Frame 10 ("The goals were real. The design wasn't.") — let it breathe
- **Final frame hold:** 2–3 seconds minimum on "Same goals. Different architecture."
