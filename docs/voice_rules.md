# Voice Rules

## Tone

- **Grounded** — anchored in real experience, not theory
- **Honest** — willing to say the uncomfortable thing plainly
- **Slightly analytical** — observes patterns, breaks things down
- **Emotionally aware** — names feelings without drowning in them
- **Calm, not hyped** — confident, not loud

The voice sounds like a smart friend who has done the work, not a coach charging $997.

---

## Style

- Simple, clear, direct language
- Short sentences. One idea per sentence.
- Structured thinking — the audience should feel like they're following a logical thread
- Zero fluff — if a word doesn't add meaning, cut it

---

## Communication Method

- **Bottom-up explanation** — start with the concrete, then the principle. Not the other way around.
- **Simplify complex ideas** — if it can't be said simply, it's not understood well enough yet
- **Combine logic + emotion** — give them a reason AND make them feel it
- **Speak like a real person** — contractions, natural rhythm, no stiff formality

---

## Avoid

| What | Why |
|------|-----|
| Generic advice | "Just be consistent" — meaningless without context |
| Preachy tone | The audience isn't here to be lectured |
| Hype language | "GAME CHANGER", "THIS WILL TRANSFORM YOUR LIFE" — instant trust killer |
| Overcomplication | Long words and jargon create distance, not authority |
| Filler transitions | "In today's video...", "So basically...", "At the end of the day..." |
| Forced positivity | "Everything happens for a reason!" — feels fake |

---

## Voice Test

Before publishing, ask:

1. Could this have been posted by a generic self-help account? → If yes, rewrite.
2. Does this sound like a person or a brand template? → Should sound like a person.
3. Would the target audience feel seen reading this? → If not, start with the feeling.
4. Is there at least one honest, specific, non-obvious insight? → If not, dig deeper.

---

## Sample Phrases That Fit

- "Here's what no one talks about..."
- "The thing I wish someone told me..."
- "It's not about X. It's about Y."
- "I used to think [wrong belief]. Here's what actually changed things."
- "This is uncomfortable but it's true..."

## Sample Phrases That Don't Fit

- "Unlock your full potential"
- "This one weird trick..."
- "Crushing it every day"
- "Your success starts NOW"
- "I'm so grateful and blessed"
