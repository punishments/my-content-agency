## Self-Recognition Hooks (Framework)

Audience sees themselves in the first 3 seconds. Open with their exact experience.

| Pattern | Example | Best for |
|---------|---------|----------|
| **You think X but it's Y** | "You think you're behind. You're actually just paying attention." | Reframe, mindset |
| **The feeling name** | "That voice that says you're not enough—that's not truth." | Vulnerability, validation |
| **The hidden pattern** | "People who thrive don't hustle more. They decide different." | Insight, POV |
| **The question they won't ask** | "What if the thing you're scared of is actually the way forward?" | Tension, possibility |
| **The thing nobody says** | "Nobody talks about this part: the doubt never fully goes away." | Honesty, authority |

## Reframe Hooks (Framework)

Shift perspective on something familiar. Create cognitive friction → resolution.

| Pattern | Example | Best for |
|---------|---------|----------|
| **And statement** | "You're doing it right AND the path looks different than you thought." | Nuance, both-and thinking |
| **Inversion** | "Don't aim for balance. Aim for what matters most." | POV, decision clarity |
| **The hidden cost** | "Certainty costs you something. Move anyway." | Growth, courage |
| **Permission reframe** | "You don't need to see the whole path. The next step is enough." | Action, permission |

## Story Structure Patterns

**Problem → Pursue → Payoff** — three beats, every piece.

### Problem Patterns

Opens with the internal struggle, not the external circumstance.

| Type | Structure | Example |
|------|-----------|---------|
| **Identity doubt** | "You do everything right. Nothing feels right." | Affect/belonging |
| **Overwhelm** | "Too many options, too much information, no clear move." | Decision paralysis |
| **Invisibility** | "You're capable but unheard. Your effort goes unseen." | Being misunderstood |
| **The gap** | "You know where you want to go. No idea how to get there." | Direction need |
| **The contradiction** | "You're successful by every measure. Still feel stuck." | Success gap |

### Pursue Patterns

The hinge point. Grounded moment where something shifted. Not advice.

| Type | Structure | Example |
|------|-----------|---------|
| **The realization** | "[X] happened. I realized [Y]." | Real event → insight |
| **The decision** | "I stopped [X]. Then [Y] changed." | Action → result |
| **The pattern** | "Every time I tried [approach], [outcome]. Then I saw..." | Repeated attempt → breakthrough |
| **The conversation** | "Someone said [thing]. I finally understood..." | External trigger → internal shift |

### Payoff Patterns

Lands somewhere new. Not a summary. The viewer leaves with a specific reframe.

| Type | Structure | Example |
|------|-----------|---------|
| **Permission** | "You don't need [X]. You need [Y]." | Clear reframe |
| **Identity** | "I'm the kind of person who [new self-definition]." | Ownership shift |
| **Framework** | "The path isn't [old belief]. It's [new structure]." | Systems thinking |
| **Invitation** | "[Action] is how you find out." | Embodied next step |

## Tone Patterns

**Grounded, honest, calm. No hype.**

| Pattern | Use | Example |
|---------|-----|---------|
| **Specific detail over generalization** | All writing | "The 3am anxiety" instead of "stress" |
| **Real over polished** | Talking, relatability | Stumble, pause, restart — leave in |
| **Question over statement** | Curiosity, permission | "What if..." instead of "You should..." |
| **Dual acknowledgment** | Nuance | "AND" instead of "BUT" |
| **Earned insight** | Authority | Grounded in experience, not theory |

## Payoff Styles by Mission

### Attract (Reach, Recognition)
**Payoff lands as:** Permission, reframe, or invitation to see self differently.

**Tone:** Specific enough to feel true. Broad enough to be shareable.

Example: "The answer isn't doing more. It's knowing which things matter."

### Position (Authority, POV)
**Payoff lands as:** Framework, new way of seeing, specific methodology.

**Tone:** Grounded expertise. Clear structure.

Example: "Clarity comes from constraints, not options. Here's how to use that."

### Nurture (Connection, Behind-the-Scenes)
**Payoff lands as:** Intimacy, validation, or the real version of something.

**Tone:** Honest, unfiltered, personal.

Example: "This is what nobody sees. This is actually how it works."

## Hook Quality Checklist

Every hook must pass ≥3 of 5:

- **Recognition:** Viewer thinks "yes, that's exactly it" by second 3
- **Tension:** Creates a question or gap that pulls the viewer forward
- **Clarity:** Immediately understandable, no jargon
- **Specificity:** Concrete details, not abstract language
- **Native Language:** Uses the exact words the audience uses internally

**If a hook passes <3:** Sharpen specificity, simplify audience language, regenerate.
